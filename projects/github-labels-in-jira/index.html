Github Labels in Jira

Chrome Extension

Supercharge your daily standup by bringing your pull requests right into the active sprint view. This extension injects the status of any associated PR along with the applied labels to give your team an up to the minute snapshot of where all your tickets stand.

When tickets are in code review, the extension will tell you who is assigned for a review, who commented, or who requested changes -- helping to facilitate your daily standups.

No more jumping through hoops to see the status of your PR's -- jump right to Github by clicking on the number!
